## Vastu Shastra Basics
Vastu harmonizes energy flow through space.  
- **Main Door:** Should face East or North for positivity.  
- **Kitchen:** Best in South-East (Agni corner). Avoid North-East.  
- **Bedroom:** South-West promotes stability in relationships.  
- **Temple / Pooja Room:** Always in North-East (Ishan corner).  
- **Bathroom:** West or North-West preferred for balance.
